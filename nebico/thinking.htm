About Us/
Our Thinking

Our pride is the TASTE of our products emphasis a HEALTHY and QUALITY food.

Nebico Private Limited is the pioneer biscuit industry of Nepal established as National Biscuit and Company Private Limited in 1964 by Late King Mahendra Bir Bikram Shah Dev at beginning. The main objective for its establishment was of manufacturing quality biscuits and confectionery in Nepal. The name that it held today was given after four year of its establishment which is NEBICO Private Limited but due to problem in the machine the production begin only from 2023BS.

In 1968, the name of the Company was changed to the present one, i.e. Nebico Pvt. Ltd. along with the capacity expansion to 15 MT per day. Britannia Industries Limited, India was in the areas of technical collaboration as well as production with NEBICO that time. Currently, the company is operating in two shifts. Annual production capacity of the company at present is around 2,400 MT.

Initially the Company has been certified for ISO 9001: 2000 and has voluntarily adopted ISO 14001:1996. Currently, the Company has adopted ISO 9001: 2008. Besides, Nebico Private Limited has subscribed to the Global Compact Company.

Nebico has been constantly serving the need of its valued consumers for decades. It has been manufacturing various brands of hard- dough and soft -dough biscuits, i.e., Glucose, Thin arrowroot, Coconut Crunches, Khaja, One to One, Digestive, Nice, and other various cream biscuits. In addition, the achievement of national and international certifications has created the highest confidence & image in its products.
